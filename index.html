Who am I?

I was raised in San Diego, so I have a strong loyalty to Southern California. Growing up, I never really had a specific interest in any given field, but by the time I got to high school, I had actually developed an interest in medicine. For two or three years, I really thought I would be a doctor, and a good one at that.

I'm good at science in general, though. I wasn't sure I wanted to limit myself to one subject, so I chose computer science when I went into college so that I could veer back to medicine if I wanted (via genetics or medical machinery) while also having flexible enough skills to do anything else if I wanted.

For now, I explore the possibilities of what I've learned as much as I can. I hope that one day, I can use these skills to impact the world in a way that I can be really proud of.
